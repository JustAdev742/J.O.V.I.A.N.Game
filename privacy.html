Global Privacy Policy

LAST UPDATED: MARCH 2026 | STATUS: ENFORCED

This Privacy Policy outlines how CodeNova Studios ("we", "our", or "us") collects, utilizes, protects, and handles your data when you interact with the JOVIAN Engine, the CodeNova Launcher, and our associated digital infrastructure (collectively, the "Services").

As a high-performance software engineering firm, CodeNova Studios is committed to absolute data minimization, cryptographic security, and transparent operational protocols. We have engineered our architecture to require as little personal data as possible, delegating specific identity operations to third-party providers.

1. Information We Collect

Our systems are designed to operate efficiently without requiring extensive personal profiles. The data we collect is limited to operational necessities:

A. Account and Authentication Data

We do not create, host, or store proprietary user accounts or raw passwords. We utilize external third-party platform providers for secure identity verification. When you authenticate via these authorized platforms, we receive:

  • A unique, anonymized Account ID (for matchmaking and save-state verification).
  • Your public display name.
  • Basic entitlement data (to verify your license to use the JOVIAN Engine or associated software).

B. Network and Telemetry Data

To ensure high-availability SLA uptime and sub-millisecond latency, our systems monitor network health. We collect:

  • Hardware Specifications: CPU/GPU models, RAM, and OS version to optimize Vulkan/DirectX 12 shader compilation.
  • Diagnostic Trace Logs: Only submitted when a user explicitly utilizes the CodeNova Launcher's internal feedback tool following a software crash.
  • Transient Network Data: See Section 3 for our decentralized networking protocols.

2. Third-Party Integrations

Our Services utilize external third-party authentication and matchmaking networks to provide cross-platform functionality. By utilizing these features, you acknowledge that your interaction with any third-party network is governed exclusively by that specific platform's privacy policy and terms of service.

CodeNova Studios does not control, and is not responsible for, the data collection or security practices of external platform partners. We receive only the minimum necessary identifiers required to facilitate gameplay. We do not process or store external account credentials within our databases.

3. Decentralized P2P Networking & IP Addresses

The JOVIAN Engine utilizes a proprietary, decentralized peer-to-peer (P2P) networking architecture to support massive-scale environments.

  • Transient Connectivity: During active matchmaking or gameplay, your IP address is temporarily shared with other clients (nodes) in your immediate instance to facilitate the P2P handshake.
  • Zero-Retention Policy: CodeNova Studios does not log, archive, or permanently store your IP address on our central servers. Once a session terminates, the routing data is purged from memory.

4. How We Use Your Information

The highly restricted data we process is used exclusively for the following technical operations:

  • Facilitating login protocols via external platform integrations.
  • Routing matchmaking requests to the lowest-latency nodes.
  • Deploying critical engine patches and updates via the CodeNova Launcher.
  • Analyzing aggregate, anonymized crash reports to finalize the C++ core architecture.
  • Maintaining the integrity of our digital ecosystem.

5. Data Security & Storage

We employ rigorous security protocols. All data in transit is encrypted using TLS 1.3. Because we do not store centralized user databases or passwords, the vector for catastrophic data breaches is fundamentally minimized by our architecture. Any localized telemetry data is stored on restricted cloud infrastructure.

6. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or monetize your data. We only share operational data with:

  • Platform Partners: Strictly for the purpose of validating authentication tokens and managing core network functionality.
  • Legal Authorities: Only when legally compelled by a valid subpoena or court order in our operating jurisdiction (New South Wales, Australia).

7. Your Rights and Control

Depending on your global jurisdiction (including GDPR and CCPA regulations), you maintain absolute rights over your data ecosystem:

  • The Right to Access: You may request a payload of the data associated with your Account ID in our systems.
  • The Right to Erasure: You may request the deletion of your historical diagnostic data.
  • Telemetry Opt-Out: Users may disable non-critical hardware telemetry directly within the CodeNova Launcher settings.

For data privacy requests, compliance inquiries, or legal concerns, please initiate a ticket with the JOVIAN Support Core.
