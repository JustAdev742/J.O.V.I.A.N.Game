1. Introduction

CodeNova Studios ("Company," "we," "us") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of our users. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and share personal data in connection with the JOVIAN Engine platform, CodeNova Launcher, and all associated online services.

2. Epic Online Services (EOS) Integration

Our platform integrates with Epic Online Services (EOS) to provide cross-platform authentication, matchmaking, voice communication, leaderboards, analytics, anti-cheat protection, and peer-to-peer signaling. When you use these features, certain data — including your EOS ProductUserId, session metadata, gameplay telemetry, and platform identifiers — may be processed by Epic Games, Inc. in accordance with their Privacy Policy. We act as a data controller for information collected through our services and Epic Games acts as an independent controller for data processed through EOS.

3. Decentralized Data Handling

The JOVIAN Engine utilizes a decentralized peer-to-peer network protocol for real-time gameplay data transmission. In this architecture, gameplay state data is transmitted directly between participating clients using encrypted channels with end-to-end encryption. Relay nodes operated by CodeNova Studios may temporarily process connection metadata (IP addresses, session tokens, latency metrics) to facilitate NAT traversal, but do not store or inspect gameplay payload data. All persistent user data (profiles, progression, inventory) is stored exclusively through EOS managed services and our secured cloud infrastructure.

4. Data Collection & Usage

We collect: (a) account registration information, (b) gameplay and performance telemetry, (c) device and hardware identifiers, (d) network diagnostics, and (e) crash reports. This data is used to operate and improve our services, ensure fair play, and comply with legal obligations. We do not sell personal data to third parties.

5. Data Retention & Security

Personal data is retained only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy. We employ industry-standard encryption (AES-256 at rest, TLS 1.3 in transit), access controls, and regular security audits to protect your information.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or port your personal data. To exercise these rights or for any privacy-related inquiries, contact us at support@joviangame.me.

7. Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Material changes will be communicated via the CodeNova Launcher and/or email notification. Continued use of our services constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
